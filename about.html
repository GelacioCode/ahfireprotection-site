Our Story

Fire Protection Built on Responsiveness

A&H Fire Protection was built around a simple expectation: customers should not have to chase down updates, estimates, or service information. We provide inspections and service for fire sprinkler systems, fire extinguishers, backflow devices, and fire alarm systems, with a focus on responsive communication and dependable follow-through.

Formed in March 2025 and backed by over 15 years of hands-on experience in commercial, industrial, and residential fire systems, A&H Fire Protection is positioned to handle the full scope of fire protection work your property needs — and to communicate clearly every step of the way.

Our service area covers the entire Front Range, from Castle Rock to Ft. Collins, with a primary focus on Downtown Denver and the surrounding metro area.
